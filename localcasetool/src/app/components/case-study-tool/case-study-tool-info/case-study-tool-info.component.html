Welcome to NEVERMORE Case study tool!

The tool is designed to provide user with a complete understanding of a case study from baseline to future scenarios through the implementation of policy-action scenarios. As a user, you will have the opportunity to evaluate climate change impacts, vulnerabilities and risks in the selected case study. After that, some policy recommendations will be provided according to your predefined hazards, expertise and interests to guide you on the implementation of policy action scenarios. As a result of the ex-ante evaluation of relevant mitigation and adaptation policy action, indicators will be provided in order to evaluate the effects of policy selection in climate change adaptation and mitigation.
