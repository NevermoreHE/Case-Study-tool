STORYLINE

Adaptation mission


Global warming is reshaping the world faster than ever, intensifying heatwaves, storms, droughts, and other climate impacts that affect communities and ecosystems, but also providing new opportunities to rethink how we live. To face this challenge, your mission is to guide a territory through a dynamic adaptation journey: reduce risks, strengthen resilience, and improve people's overall quality of life.

As you navigate each challenge, you will make strategic decisions, implement adaptation measures, and balance resources wisely. Every choice counts. By protecting ecosystems, upgrading infrastructure, and supporting vulnerable sectors, you will guide your territory toward a safer and more sustainable future.

The goal is clear: Transform uncertainty into action, risk into resilience, and today’s challenges into tomorrow’s opportunities.

QUICK RULES

🎯 Round setup
Adjust the hazard level based on your mitigation mission scenario. The new value updates automatically.

🌪️ Climate event
Pick 1 event card per round — its effects on each sector will apply instantly.

🛠️ Adaptation measures
Play up to 2 measure cards per round. Their costs and risk reductions are applied immediately.

💰 Budget & timing
If a measure has lag, effects start later. When the lifetime ends, impacts and co-benefits are halved, but OPEX continues.

⚖️ Risk rules
Risk per sector can’t drop below 15 or exceed 90. Manage your budget wisely to keep balance!
